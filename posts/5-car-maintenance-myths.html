5 Car Maintenance Myths You Should Stop Believing

There are many car maintenance tips out there — but not all are true. Let’s bust some myths:

  • Myth 1: You need to change oil every 3,000 miles.
    ✅ Most modern cars can go 5,000–7,500 miles easily.
  • Myth 2: Premium fuel = better performance.
    ✅ Not unless your car specifically requires it.
  • Myth 3: Warm up your car before driving.
    ✅ Modern engines are fine after 30 seconds.
  • Myth 4: Brake fluid lasts forever.
    ✅ It absorbs moisture and needs to be changed every 2–3 years.
  • Myth 5: More frequent servicing is always better.
    ✅ Stick to the manual. Over-servicing = wasting money.
